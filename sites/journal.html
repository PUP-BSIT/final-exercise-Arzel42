  • End of The Beginnings (12/31/2023)

    Beneath the shade of midnight's embrace, A year departs, leaving its trace. In the book of time, a chapter ends, Yet, a new one starts, with lessons to transcend. The canvas blank, an unwritten story, New beginnings bathed in the dawn's glory. The tapestry of memories, both old and new, A mosaic of experiences, a kaleidoscope view. In the garden of challenges, seeds of growth, Each trial faced, a testament to both Resilience and courage, blooming in the heart, A symphony of lessons, a work of art.

    As we welcome the new year, I can't help but look back on my college days. Remembering the mix of good and not-so-good times, it feels like a journey through different worlds. College has been a time of both challenges and carefree moments. Late-night studying, facing tough exams, and celebrating small victories with friends have all been part of this adventure. These experiences have shaped me, making me more resilient and determined. But there's also a sense of nostalgia for the days when money wasn't a worry, and time seemed endless. I miss the simplicity of those times. Now, standing on the brink of the future, I feel a bit uncertain but excited. The upcoming transition from college to adulthood feels like a big step. It's a bit scary, but it's also a chance for growth and new discoveries. I'm realizing that time is precious, and I'm at a crossroads between looking back and looking forward. The memories, whether they bring a smile or a lesson, are coming along with me into the new year. They're like a guide, helping me navigate the unknown path that lies ahead. Here's to stepping into the next chapter with the lessons learned and the hope for new adventures.
